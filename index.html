I’m a Research Fellow, currently with Susan Stepney’s Nonstandard Computation Group. My research interests include:

  • Metaheuristics
  • Search-Based Software Engineering
  • Program Induction and Synthesis
  • Cognitive/Agent-based Architectures.
  • Symbolic Computation
  • Rewriting systems/Automatic Groups

Professional Roles

  • Fellow of the Institute of Mathematics and its Applications.
  • Journal Reviewer: Journal of Heuristics, Cognitive Computation, Annals of Operations Research, Knowledge-Based Systems, Journal of Location Based Services, Automated Software Engineering, Journal of Systems and Software, IEEE Transactions on Evolutionary Computation, IEEE Transactions on Computational Intelligence and AI in Games, Natural Computing, Genetic Programming and Evolvable Machines.
  • Program Committee Member: GECCO 2011-2015, PPSN 2012+2014, MISTA 2013, ISCIS 2013, CEC 2013-2015, SSBSE 2014-2015, NaBIC 2015.
  • Co-organizer for GECCO Workshops on:
    • Evolutionary Computation for the Automated Design of Algorithms, 2011-2014
    • MetaDeeP - Metaheuristic Design Patterns Workshop, 2014.
  • Member of the IEEE task force on hyper-heuristics.
